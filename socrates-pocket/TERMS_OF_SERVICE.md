## 2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

Socrates Pocket is a reflective self-inquiry app inspired by the Socratic method. The App allows you to:

- Share a thought and receive AI-generated Socratic questions in return
- Move through short conversational sessions (3 to 7 questions per session) in one of several mentorship themes
- See a reasoning chain summarizing your own thinking at the end of each session
- Save insights to a private on-device Journal
- Access additional features through a Socrates Pocket Pro subscription

The App uses large-language-model (LLM) technology provided by third-party AI processors (see our Privacy Policy). **The questions, reasoning chains, and any other text produced inside the App are generated by AI and may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for your situation.** See Sections 9 and 10 for limitations.
