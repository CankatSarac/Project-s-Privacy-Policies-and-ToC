# SOCRATES POCKET PRIVACY POLICY

**Effective Date:** April 24, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 24, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Socrates Pocket ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and have designed this app with a privacy-first philosophy. This Privacy Policy explains how Socrates Pocket collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you use our mobile application ("App"), and — importantly — what we do **not** collect.

Socrates Pocket is a Socratic-method reflection app. You share a thought, receive Socratic questions, and arrive at your own insight. The app is designed so that your reflections remain **on your device**, tied to no account, under no login, linked to no profile.

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 No Account, No Profile

Socrates Pocket **does not require you to create an account**. We do not collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Your phone number
- Your username or social handles
- Your date of birth
- Your photo, avatar, or biographical information
- Any contact list, calendar, or social graph

There is no login screen because there is no account.

### 1.2 Content You Write in the App

When you share a thought or answer a question within a Socratic session, that text is **processed by a third-party AI provider** (see Section 3.2) so the app can generate the next question and, at the end of the session, your reasoning chain. This processing happens in real time and does not result in the content being stored by us.

The following stays **only on your device**, in your device's encrypted local storage, and is never transmitted to us:

- Your opening thoughts
- Your answers to Socratic questions
- The AI's Socratic questions to you
- Your reasoning chain and insight summary for each session
- The timestamp, theme, and question count of each session
- Your selected theme preference
- Your daily usage counter (for free-tier gating)

If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

### 1.3 Purchases and Subscriptions

When you purchase a Socrates Pocket Pro subscription, the purchase is processed by **Apple** (App Store) or **Google** (Play Store) and managed through **RevenueCat**, our subscription infrastructure provider. For this purpose, we or our providers may collect:

- An anonymous user identifier generated by RevenueCat (not linked to your real name, email, or any personally identifying information)
- The fact that a subscription was purchased, renewed, cancelled, or refunded
- The product identifier and pricing tier
- Platform and country of purchase (as reported by Apple/Google)

This information is used solely to deliver your Pro entitlement, support purchase restoration, and comply with tax and billing requirements.

### 1.4 Automatically Collected Information

We do **not** run any third-party analytics SDK, advertising SDK, attribution SDK, or crash-reporting tool that transmits identifiers. We do **not** collect:

- Device advertising identifier (IDFA / AAID)
- Your IP address
- Precise or approximate geolocation
- App usage analytics
- Crash or performance telemetry
- Clickstream data

### 1.5 Crisis-Safety Feature

Socrates Pocket includes a crisis-detection feature that displays mental-health resources (such as the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline in the United States, the Crisis Text Line, and the International Association for Suicide Prevention directory) if the text you type matches a small, locally-evaluated list of crisis keywords. This check runs entirely on your device. Nothing about whether crisis resources were shown is transmitted off your device.

## 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

The limited information described in Section 1.2 and Section 1.3 is used to:

- Generate the next Socratic question in your session
- Generate your reasoning chain at the end of a session
- Store your session history on your own device so you can return to it in the Journal
- Deliver and manage Socrates Pocket Pro subscriptions
- Restore previous purchases when you reinstall the app
- Comply with legal, tax, and billing obligations

We do **not** use your content for marketing, advertising, training AI models, profiling, or any purpose beyond those listed above.

## 3. INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

### 3.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade any data — personal or otherwise — to any party. We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketers.

### 3.2 AI Processors

To generate Socratic questions and reasoning chains, the content you type in a session is transmitted in real time to one of the following AI service providers:

- **Rork AI Toolkit** (primary provider, on behalf of Socrates Pocket)
- **Google Gemini** (fallback)
- **Anthropic Claude** (fallback)
- **OpenAI GPT** (fallback)

These providers receive only the text necessary to produce a response (your current thought, the active conversation context, and the system prompt). They do not receive your device identifier, RevenueCat ID, name, email, or any other identifier tying the content to you as a person. Each provider operates under its own privacy terms and commercial agreements in which they commit to not using API content for training their general-purpose models:

- Google Gemini via API — [ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms)
- Anthropic Claude via API — [anthropic.com/legal/privacy](https://anthropic.com/legal/privacy)
- OpenAI API — [openai.com/enterprise-privacy](https://openai.com/enterprise-privacy)
- Rork AI Toolkit — [rork.com](https://rork.com)

### 3.3 Payment and Subscription Providers

- **Apple** — for iOS purchases (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
- **Google** — for Android purchases (governed by Google's privacy policy)
- **RevenueCat** — for cross-platform subscription management ([revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy))

### 3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or a valid government request, or to:

- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent illegal activities
- Ensure user safety

Because the bulk of your reflective content never leaves your device, there is typically nothing for us to disclose in response to such a request.

### 3.5 Business Transfers

If Socrates Pocket is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, any information we hold may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change and of any resulting changes to this Privacy Policy.

## 4. DATA SECURITY

We implement privacy-by-design and industry-standard safeguards:

- On-device data is stored in the operating system's sandboxed storage, protected by the device's disk encryption
- Network requests to AI and subscription providers are transmitted over TLS (HTTPS)
- The app does not operate a backend database of user content; there is no server-side store to breach
- We regularly review our dependencies for security advisories

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. DATA RETENTION

Because we do not store your session content on any server, **retention is controlled by you**:

- Session history, preferences, and usage counters live on your device until you delete the app or clear its data in your device settings
- Subscription records held by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat are retained for as long as required by their respective policies and applicable tax law

You can erase all on-device data at any time by uninstalling the app or using your operating system's "Clear Storage" feature for Socrates Pocket.
