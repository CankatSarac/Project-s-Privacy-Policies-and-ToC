## 2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

NameStory is a mobile application that allows users to:

- Type a name and receive statistical demographic predictions (age, gender, likely nationalities)
- Read the meaning, origin, and cultural context of a name pulled from public encyclopedic sources
- Save a personal history of names they have looked up
- Optionally sync that history across devices via Google, Apple, or email sign-in
- Process up to 100 names at a time in batch mode

All core features are free to use. No advertising is shown inside the App. Predictions are produced by external statistical APIs and are estimates, not facts.

## 7. NATURE OF PREDICTIONS AND DISCLAIMER OF ACCURACY

NameStory's predictions are **statistical estimates**, not facts. They are generated by third-party public APIs that use aggregated population-level data. In particular:

- **Age predictions** reflect the average age of registered users of a name in census and naming-registry data, not the actual age of any specific person.
- **Gender predictions** reflect the distribution of a name across male, female, and other gender markers in public records, not the identity of any specific person.
- **Nationality predictions** reflect the geographic distribution of a name in public records, not the citizenship, ethnicity, or heritage of any specific person.
- **Meanings and origins** are sourced from Wikipedia and the public domain; they may be incomplete, culturally specific, or occasionally inaccurate.

You must not use NameStory's predictions to make assumptions about specific real individuals, and you must not present its output as authoritative.
